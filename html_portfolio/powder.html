Powder

A snowboarding magazine for women, by women.

Powder is a concept, a project just for fun.
Mission Statement - Female snowboarders are underrepresented. Powder’s goal is to fix that. Powder is a revolutionary magazine with world-wide circulation, written by womaen, for woman, providing an in-depth look at the world of WOMEN’S snowboarding.
