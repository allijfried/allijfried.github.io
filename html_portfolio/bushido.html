The Bushido School of Karate

Bushido School of Karate, wanted to take their website to the next stage by totally revamping it. Their current site was not user friendly, finding information was a challenge. Because it was a pre-existing site, the content was already created. The initial focus was to organize all of its content to then create a clear website. Navigation Exploration

The ideal target audience for Bushido School of Karate are fitness oriented adults of all ages as well children and their parents/guardians. Because of the wide age range the navigation had to be very clear. Below are a couple examples of the many navigation options explored.

In an attempt to make the site mobile first, the experimenting began with a hamburger navigation. Trying a couple different ways to implement it and how the navigation would appear.

Final Choice for Navigation

After more thought, I realized to appeal to the wide age range, and should therefore stick with a more standard navigation layout. This would help the audience understand the sites flow with ease.

As the user scrolls down the page the navigation condenses to take up less real estate on the page.
