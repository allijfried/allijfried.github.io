This was a project for Roomi, a startup, whose purpose is to help people “break free from traditional lease and find flexible, affordable co-living solutions” with a “peer-to-peer marketplace”. Roomi asked us to create a tool that solved the question of “who should I live with and where should I live”. With just 6 weeks and a small team, we created Bunki. Bunki is a website where the user takes a short and fun quiz that puts their answers through our algorithm and gives them their perfect roommate and neighborhood from Roomi’s listings.
