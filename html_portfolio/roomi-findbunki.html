Roomi is a startup whose goal is to help people “break free from the traditional lease and find flexible, affordable co-living solutions”. This new firm presented a challenge to our team asking us to help develop a creative mechanism for matching the Roomi user with ideal housing and roommate opportunities. Our small team, in just six weeks, created a website, which we called Bunki. Bunki acquires user information through a short, user-friendly quiz, then analyzes the input through a unique algorithm based upon Roomi’s own listings. At the end of the process, the user is provided a “perfect” roommate and housing match, based on many factors, including, but not limited to, neighborhood, personality traits and budget. Bunki is an intuitive, simple and creative means to meet the client’s needs.
